Reaction Time Comparison by Metal

Zinc Reaction Times

Zinc reacted fastest when cleaned (45s), slower when untouched (73s), and slowest with skin contact (110s)

Steel Reaction Times

Steel showed the most dramatic difference: cleaned (65s) vs skin contact (160s) - 2.5x slower!

Magnesium Reaction Times

Magnesium, being most reactive, showed faster overall times but still slower with skin contact (35s vs 15s)
